NEWLINE

To celebrate hackerspace.gent and the hacker community once a year, we organize the "Newline" hacker conference. A newline character marks the end of a line and moves the cursor to the next one. It's a sign of progress and it gives you a whole new line to be filled. Let's join for some short accessible talks, warm food, cold beer, and Club Mate.

Call for Papers is now OPEN!

The hacker community is a diverse one. We welcome people from all walks of life, and we promote mutual respect. Whether you identify as a grape, wear a tail or are a time traveller, feel welcome! Anything goes. Come talk about your ketchup addiction, show off your ballet moves or come tell us why you should be the next president of the cheese club.

We are an open forum, a listening audience. We love technology but we sure would also love to learn more about any kind of hobby you might have. If you need space or resources or a room filled with people, just let us know through the participation form.

Submit your proposal at cfp.hackerspace.gent

The Newline 2025 Schedule

Here’s a sneak peek at some of the exciting talks and workshops:

Meshtastic: Off-grid communication for everyone

Discover how this open-source project enables long-range, decentralized communication without relying on the internet.

MIOTY LPWAN: Who is the champion of Massive IoT applications?

Explore MIOTY, an emerging LPWAN technology designed for robust, energy-efficient IoT communication.

Driving Trains Remotely using Open-Source Software

How enthusiasts and professionals are using open-source tools to control model and real-world railway systems.

Scanning MX Records of European Municipalities – Lessons Learned

A deep dive into the email security of European governments and what we discovered from large-scale MX record scanning.

A Tour of the European Open-Source Online Platforms

Explore key open-source platforms driving innovation and collaboration across Europe.

Copy-on-Write (CoW) Filesystems are Awesome!

Learn why CoW filesystems like Btrfs and ZFS are changing the way we handle storage and data integrity.
